PIGDICE GAME

RULES

In this game 2 players go head on as each player tries to attain a max score of 100 points by rolling dice.

Here are rules that the game follows;

  • When a player rolls dice and the outcome is between 2 and 6 it is added to their turn score.
  • when the outcome of the dice is 1,the turn score for the player becomes zero and the chance passes to the second player to play.
  • When a player chooses to "HOLD" it means that he/she has passed on their chance to roll dice and thus their turn score is added to their total score.
  • The player who manages to ultimately reach 100 points first wins the game and the two can start another game.
